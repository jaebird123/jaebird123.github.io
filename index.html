Jesse Eaton

Biologist. Statistician. Computer Scientist.

I am a graduate student at Carnegie Mellon University studying computational biology. In Professor Russell Schwartz's lab, I write code to help diagnose cancer. We detect the severity of a tumor by first "unmixing" a bulk tumor sample into subclones (different cell types), building a phylogeny (or tree of life) for the tumor, and finally using features from phylogentic features for each patient to predict the severity of the cancer.
